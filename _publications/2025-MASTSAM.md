Combined Mast3R with the SAM models as a member of the team that developed MastSAM, a new method for performing semantically consistent segmentation across consecutive video frames. Tested MastSAM on the DAVIS dataset, achieving a 92.32% consistency, significantly surpassing BYOCL's 78.73%, while enhancing efficiency by nearly 50%.
